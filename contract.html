INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

Agreement made                        (“Anniversary Date”)  by and between Giddy Digs and                      (“Independent Contractor”)

Independent Contractor is an independent contractor willing to provide certain skills and abilities to the Giddy Digs that the Giddy Digs has need for.

In consideration of the mutual terms, conditions and covenants hereinafter set forth, Giddy Digs and Independent Contractor agree as follows:

Services to Be Performed

Independent Contractor agrees to sell and/or list real estate only with Giddy Digs.  Independent Contractor will not be treated as an employee with respect to the services performed by Independent Contractor as a real estate agent for federal tax purposes.

Compensation


In consideration for the services to be performed by Independent Contractor, Giddy Digs agrees to make payment to the Independent Contractor for completed transactions pursuant to the following compensation structure options (to be completed and initialled by both parties):

_______Option 1: Independent Contractor will pay a flat monthly fee of $           .  This monthly fee will be paid by direct deposit, which Independent Contractor will set up within five (5) business days of the date of this Agreement, to be paid on a recurring monthly basis no later than the 5th day of of each month.  If Independent Contractor is more than sixty (60) days late in paying this monthly fee, Independent Contractor will automatically be switched to a 20% commission rate structure, as set forth in Option 3, no other options will apply,  and all amounts owing will be deducted from Independent Contractor’s next commission and/or compensation.

_______Option 2: Independent Contractor will pay a fee of $          per transaction, meaning each commission or payment received by the Giddy Digs.  This transaction fee will be automatically deducted from any payment/commission received by the Giddy Digs prior to Independent Contractor receiving compensation.  At a minimum, Independent Contractor will pay $500 per year to the Giddy Digs. In the event that Independent Contractor has not met their $500 minimum by their Anniversary Date, the Independent Contractor will be required to pay to the Giddy Digs the difference between already paid transaction fees and $500. Payment of this annual minimum amount will be paid to the Giddy Digs no later than five (5) business days after the Anniversary Date.  

 _______Option 3: Independent Contractor will pay a fee equal to                percent of each  commission or payment received by the Giddy Digs.  This commission amount will be automatically deducted from any payment received by the Giddy Digs prior to Independent Contractor receiving compensation.  At a minimum, Independent Contractor will pay $500 per year to the Giddy Digs. In the event that Independent Contractor has not met their $500 minimum by their Anniversary Date, the Independent Contractor will be required to pay to the Giddy Digs the difference between already paid transaction fees and $500. Payment of this annual minimum amount will be paid to the Giddy Digs no later than five (5) business days after the Anniversary Date.

 _______Option 4: Independent Contractor’s total commission paid to Giddy Digs will be capped at $                           .  Said cap will restart at $0 each year on Independent Contractor’s Anniversary Date.  

 _______Option 5: Any commission or payment received by Giddy Digs for a referral fee, where the Independent Contractor is not named as the listing or sellers’ agent but is solely paid on a referral basis, will pay Giddy Digs $          per referral agreement and no other fees will be charged.

Regardless of which compensation structure is selected, Independent Contractor will pay $100 per transaction. This fee applies even when Independent Contractor selects a compensation structure including a cap (Option 4).  This fee will be deducted from every transaction prior to the Independent Contractor receiving compensation.

In the event that Independent Contractor has failed to meet their minimum contribution requirements as set forth above, as of their Anniversary Date, Giddy Digs may terminate this Agreement as set forth below.

Any changes to Independent Contractor’s compensation must be agreed upon in writing and signed by both parties. Any changes to Independent Contractor’s compensation structure can only be renegotiated before the Anniversary Date and any new compensation structure will commence on Anniversary Date, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

 
Independent Contractor shall have no right to compensation based on the number of hours worked.


Expenses


Independent Contractor shall be responsible for all expenses incurred while performing services under this Agreement. This includes license fees, memberships, and dues; automobile and other travel expenses; meals and entertainment; insurance premiums; and all salary, expenses, and other compensation paid to employees or contract personnel the Independent Contractor hires to complete the work under this Agreement.


Sales Office


Giddy Digs agrees to provide Independent Contractor with the use, equally with other Independent Contractors, of all the common areas (conference room, break room, balconies) of the Giddy Digs offices located at 1031 South Bluff Street (“Executive Suites”). Said common areas will be used for meeting with clients, training, or other meetings conducted by the Giddy Digs and for no other purpose without prior approval of Giddy Digs.  Independent Contractor is expected to maintain their own office space and will not be provided office space.  Independent Contractor will schedule conference room time directly with the receptionist of the Executive Suites for meetings with clients.  Independent Contractor will be given an access card for a one time fee of $10.  Independent Contractor may opt to set up with a copy code for printing services and will be charged for all copy expenses in excess of $15 per month.

Independent Contractor is expected to know and follow all rules set forth by the owners of the Executive Suites regarding use and access to the building, including parking rules.


Independent Contractor Status


Independent Contractor is an independent contractor, not Giddy Digs’s employee. Independent Contractor's employees or contract personnel are not Giddy Digs’s employees. Independent Contractor and Giddy Digs agree to the following rights consistent with an independent contractor relationship.
•        Independent Contractor has the right to perform services not including activities in the capacity of or requiring real estate license, for others during the term of this Agreement.
•        Independent Contractor has the sole right to control and direct the means, manner, and method by which the services required by this Agreement will be performed.
•        Subject to any restrictions on Contractor's sales territory contained in this Agreement, Independent Contractor has the right to perform the services required by this Agreement at any location or time.
•        Independent Contractor has the right to hire assistants as subcontractors, or to use employees to provide the services required by this Agreement.


Business Permits, Certificates, and Licenses


Independent Contractor has complied with all federal, state, and local laws requiring business permits, certificates, and licenses required to carry out the services to be performed under this Agreement.


Independent Contractor represents and warrants that Independent Contractor is a licensed real estate Independent Contractor in good standing, having been licensed by the State of Utah on  _____[date]_____. Independent Contractor will provide the Giddy Digs a copy of the agent’s license.


State and Federal Taxes

Giddy Digs will not:
•        withhold FICA (Social Security and Medicare taxes) from Independent Contractor's payments or make FICA payments on Independent Contractor's behalf
•        make state or federal unemployment compensation contributions on Independent Contractor's behalf, or
•        withhold state or federal income tax from Independent Contractor's payments.
Independent Contractor shall pay all taxes incurred while performing services under this Agreement—including all applicable income taxes and, if Independent Contractor is not a corporation, self-employment (Social Security) taxes.

Fringe Benefits

Independent Contractor understands that neither Independent Contractor nor Independent Contractor's employees or contract personnel are eligible to participate in any employee pension, profit sharing, health, vacation pay, sick pay, or other fringe benefit plan of Giddy Digs.


Workers' Compensation


Giddy Digs shall not obtain workers' compensation insurance on behalf of Independent Contractor or Independent Contractor's employees. If Independent Contractor hires employees to perform any work under this Agreement, Independent Contractor will cover them with workers' compensation insurance to the extent required by law and provide Giddy Digs with a certificate of workers' compensation insurance before the employees begin the work.


Unemployment Compensation

Giddy Digs shall make no state or federal unemployment compensation payments on behalf of Independent Contractor or Independent Contractor's employees or contract personnel. Independent Contractor will not be entitled to these benefits in connection with work performed under this Agreement.


Insurance


Giddy Digs shall maintain insurance coverage in an adequate amount to cover specific acts committed by Independent Contractor while performing services under this Agreement.

 
Independent Contractor shall indemnify and hold Giddy Digs harmless from any loss or liability arising from performing services under this Agreement. This includes any claim for injuries or damages caused by Independent Contractor while traveling in Independent Contractor's automobile and performing services under this Agreement.


Terminating the Agreement


Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason, with or without cause, at any time by giving written notice to the other party of the intent to terminate.

Exclusive Agreement


This is the entire Agreement between Independent Contractor and Giddy Digs.


Severability


If any provision of this Agreement is determined to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain in full force if the essential provisions of this Agreement for Giddy Digs and Independent Contractor remain valid, binding, and enforceable.

Resolving Disputes

If a dispute arises under this Agreement, the parties agree first to try to resolve the dispute with the help of a mutually agreed upon Utah court-certified mediator.  Any costs and fees other than attorney fees associated with the mediation shall be shared equally by the parties.


If a dispute is not resolved within 30 days after either party request mediation, and after a good faith effort by the parties to resolve the dispute through mediation, any party may take the matter to court.

Applicable Law


This Agreement will be governed by the laws of the State of Utah.


Notices


All notices and other communications in connection with this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be considered given as follows:
•        when delivered personally to the recipient's address as stated on this Agreement
•        three days after being deposited in the United States mail, with postage prepaid to the recipient's address as stated on this Agreement, or
•        when sent by email to the last email address of the recipient known to the person giving notice. Notice is effective upon receipt provided that a duplicate copy of the notice is promptly given by first class mail, or the recipient delivers a written confirmation of receipt.


No Partnership


This Agreement does not create a partnership relationship. Independent Contractor does not have authority to enter into contracts on Giddy Digs' behalf outside Independent Contractors limited agency with Giddy Digs and in accordance with all rules and regulations of the Utah Division of Real Estate.
